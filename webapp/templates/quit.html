The aim of this research is to assess whether the complexity of the scenario can influence user’s perceptions on the quality of the recommendations and of the explanations which whom these are presented. You were presented with several scenarios, more or less complex in terms of group member’s agreement on the possible choices. We aim at understanding if this has an impact on the evaluations of the provided recommendations.
