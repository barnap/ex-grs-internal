Music Group Recommendations

Thanks for your participation in this study. In this study, we will first ask you to provide demographic information. Then, we will ask you to provide some restaurant names, matching some specific evaluations (in a range from 1 to 5). Furthermore, we will ask you to provide the names of 8 friends, that will be used in the subsequent scenarios. After these steps, we will ask you to evaluate six group recommendation scenarios, where you and some of your friends are supposed to decide for a restaurant to go for dinner. Read carefully the provided instructions before answering the questions. Finally, you will be asked to suggest two restaurants for a new group.
