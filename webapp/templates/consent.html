Recommendations to Groups

The purpose of this study is to analyze user’s perceptions about group recommendations presented in different ways. Francesco Barile, Assistant Professor at Maastricht University (Department of Advanced Computing Sciences - DACS) and Pierre Hurlin (check affiliation) are conducting this study.
Your participation in this research study is voluntary. You may choose not to participate. If you decide to participate in this research survey, you may withdraw at any time. If you do not complete the survey, we will treat it as a withdrawal and your answers thus far will not be used.
The procedure involves evaluating several depicted scenarios and answering to a survey. The whole experiment should have a duration of about 10 minutes. What to expect?
  • You will be asked to provide demographic info (age group and gender).
  • Then, you will be asked to evaluate six presented group recommendation scenarios.
  • Finally, you will be asked to evaluate a final scenario.
All data is stored in a password protected electronic format. We will not store any information that will personally identify you, including IP addresses. The results of this study will be used for the creation of scholarly publication. Furthermore, a public dataset will be generated and made available to the scientific community; in this case, only anonymized data will be included in such dataset.
